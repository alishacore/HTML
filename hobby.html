My fav Hobbies

Poetry & Book Reading are my fav hobbies

Poetry: The Language of My Soul

Poetry, for me, is more than just words—it’s the rhythm of my emotions, the melody of my thoughts, and the echo of my dreams. When I write poetry, I feel like I’m pouring my heart onto the page, shaping feelings into verses that speak louder than speech.

Why I love poetry:

  1. It helps me express emotions I can’t say out loud.
  2. It connects me to beauty, depth, and imagination.
  3. It gives me a sense of peace and creative freedom.

How I write poetry:

  1. I write when I’m alone, inspired by a mood, a moment, or a memory.
  2. I often read Urdu poets like Faiz, Parveen Shakir, and Jaun Elia to spark inspiration.
  3. I jot down verses in my notebook or phone, and sometimes share them with close friends.

Book Reading: A Journey Through Worlds

Books are my escape, my teachers, and my companions . Whether it’s a romantic Urdu novel or a thought-provoking classic, I lose myself in the pages and find new perspectives, emotions, and adventures.

Why I love reading:

  1. It expands my imagination and understanding of the world.
  2. It improves my language, thinking, and empathy.
  3. - It gives me comfort, inspiration, and joy.

How I read books:

  1. I read at night before sleeping or during quiet afternoons.
  2. Sometimes I read PDFs on my phone, other times I hold a physical book.
  3. I highlight favorite quotes or keep them in a journal to revisit later.
